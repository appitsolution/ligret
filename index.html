Areas of trading

The main direction of trading activity is purchase and sale of electricity, natural gas, agricultural products and technical equipment. The headquarters of the company is located in Vienna, which allows us to quickly and flexibly respond to the requests of customers in Eastern Europe.

The company's central office is located in Kyiv and is responsible for the company's operational activities.


Values and principles

Thanks to the joint and coordinated work of the team of traders, Ligret occupies a strong and stable position both on the Ukrainian and international markets. Thorough daily analysis of the market allows us to achieve effective results, and mutual trust guarantees continuous development at all levels of our activity.

Satisfied customers

The satisfaction of our customers is our main priority! Remaining open to the wishes and suggestions of customers, we provide qualified services, effectively solving the assigned tasks.


Priceless human factor

Ligret's success depends on our workers. This is the key pillar of the company, an invaluable human factor that ensures the company development with its own loyalty, purposefulness, competence and respect. 


Only the best products, solutions and professional service at the best price
